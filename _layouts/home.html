About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at OSLAB, KAIST, under the supervision of Prof. Youjip Won. I earned my M.S. at KAIST, also under the guidance of Prof. Youjip Won, and my B.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) from UNIST, graduating Summa Cum Laude. My research interests lie in filesystems and storage, with a particular focus on journaling techniques. I am passionate about building crash-consistent, scalable, and high-performance filesystem.
